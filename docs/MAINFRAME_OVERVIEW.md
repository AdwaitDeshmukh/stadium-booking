# Mainframe Overview

This document serves as a beginner-friendly introduction to mainframes, DB2, and COBOL. 

## What is a Mainframe?
Mainframes are powerful computers primarily used by large organizations. They handle vast amounts of data and support multiple users simultaneously. Unlike personal computers, mainframes are optimized for data processing and transactions.

## Introduction to DB2
DB2 is a database management system that runs on mainframes. It allows users to store, retrieve, and manage data efficiently.

## What is COBOL?
COBOL (Common Business Oriented Language) is one of the oldest programming languages, designed for business applications. It is known for its readability and reliability.

## Conclusion
Mainframes, DB2, and COBOL are crucial for many large-scale applications, providing stability and efficiency in processing large volumes of data.
